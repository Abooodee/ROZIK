The All New IPhone 14 Pro



IPhone 14 Pro

The iPhone 14 Pro 5G Dual SIM comes with a 6.1 inch touchscreen with 460PPI. It packs a 48-megapixel pro camera with main ultra wide system and a 12-megapixel selfie-camera with TrueDepth with autofocus. This is all powered by the Apple A16 Bionic (4 nm) chipset and 6GB of RAM.

Apple’s latest and most beautiful device also comes with Gorilla glass on the front and back with a stainless steel frame, like its predecessor. For more details and features of the Apple iPhone 14 Pro, please read below, as we bring you all the latest specifications and designs of the smartphone, with its official pricing. So, let us begin.

Features

To begin with, the Apple iPhone 14 Pro’s design is similar to the last generation of iPhones and is identical to the Apple iPhone 13 Pro. The smartphone comes with a Gorilla Glas at both the front and the back, with a stainless steel frame to protect the smartphone from accidental damage. The back glass texture feels great on both sides, and a ceramic shield coating is also there on the front. The Apple iPhone 14 Pro is IP68 certified as well, meaning it can withstand water and dust with ease.
